Braille Transcriber

Our transcriber handles the two standardized forms of English Braille; Grade 1 and Grade 2. Grade 1 braille is used by most beginners, and is an almost exact letter-to-letter transcription. This websiteâ€™s Grade 1 braille is completely accurate.

Grade 2 braille is used by most experienced braille readers, using abbreviations and contractions to speed up the reading process. It must be said that currently, a completely accurate Grade 2 braille transcription can only be done by a human transcriber, but this transcriber has been our best attempt at an accurate transcription, including all formalized contractions and abbreviations, with sub rules for uninterrupted syllables and pronunciation.

We hope this can be helpful in making texts more accessible in ways other than audio (which can be slower than reading), and please leave a message on the Contact page if you notice an obvious error, and we will try to fix the mistake. Thank you!
